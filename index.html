Sebar WIB

Familiarity Night, often referred to as Makrab, is a special event held annually on our campus. The event aims to strengthen the bond between new students and senior students. In an atmosphere full of spirit and fun, Makrab presents an opportunity for participants to build relationships, celebrate friendships, and share memorable experiences..

Komunal

In a college environment, especially for new students, the concept of communal becomes one of the main cornerstones to build a strong foundation in their journey as part of the college community. This concept lays the foundation for brotherhood, togetherness, and social networks that not only influence their academic experience, but also play an important role in personal growth and development during their studies.

Sosil

The concept of socializing on campus is all about how new students feel welcome, have positive social experiences, and form their social identity within the campus community. This is an important part of the college experience, which not only affects their social aspect, but also provides valuable lessons about interacting with others, managing time, and experiencing college life more broadly.
